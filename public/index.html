Where to Find Your Fan's ID Plate

The ID plate is a small metal or plastic label showing the manufacturer, model number, and specifications.

Wall-Mounted Fans

Behind the front grille or on the back of the unit. You may need to remove the cover to see it.

Inline / Duct Fans

On the motor casing, visible from the side of the unit.
